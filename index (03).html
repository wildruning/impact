Step 2 · Ask Targeted Questions
Specific Questions Unlock
Specific Criticism
1
What assumptions here could be wrong?
2
If this fails in 6 months, what's the most likely reason?
3
What would a skeptical [owner / lender / inspector] say is the biggest risk?
4
What's the weakest part that's easy to miss?
→ Pick 2–3 after sharing any plan or idea
Step 2 · The Finisher
Make AI Grade Itself
Switches AI from "be helpful" mode → "find problems" mode
Prompt
"Analyze that response. Rate it from 1 to 100 for how genuinely critical and useful it was. Identify the three weakest points, then rewrite the feedback fixing those weaknesses."
Take your improved argument → new conversation for Step 3

The Skeptical Persona
Prompt
"You are a skeptical [fill in the blank]. I'm going to share my plan. Your job is to tear it apart — find every weakness, question every assumption, and tell me exactly where this falls short. Don't soften anything. Make your feedback specific and actionable."
