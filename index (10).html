Layer 1
Set the Rules
Before you prompt
1
Move 1
Defining Terms
Paste this into your custom instructions or Project
Custom Instructions
For simple questions, answer in 1–2 sentences. For medium questions, give me 3–5 sentences. For complex questions, give me one short summary paragraph followed by up to 5 bullet points. Start directly with the answer. Stop after the last relevant fact.
Move 2
Positive Instructions
Replace every "don't" with a "do"
× Negation
"Don't write long introductions"
"Don't use filler words"
"Don't add unnecessary context"
✓ Direction
"Start directly with the answer"
"Use short declarative sentences"
"Lead with the most important point"
Layer 2
Constrain the Prompt
During — In the prompt itself
2
Move 3
Force a Format That Can't Hold Fluff
Open-ended prompt
I run a 30-person company doing about 5 million a year. I need to grow our marketing but I'm not sure whether to hire someone in-house or use an outside agency. What should I consider?
→ 400–600 words of prose
Format-constrained prompt
I run a 30-person company doing about 5 million a year. I need to grow our marketing but I'm not sure whether to hire someone in-house or use an outside agency. What should I consider? Answer only as a comparison table with rows for annual cost, time to ramp up, level of control, scalability, and biggest risk.
→ Clean, structured table

Fluff-Proof Formats

Layer 3
Tighten After
After you get the response
3
Move 4
The Sharpen Command
Don't rewrite your prompt. Just reply with one line.
Reply with
Cut length by 50%, remove all generalizations, short punchy sentences only.
Move 5
The Filter Pass
For deep research that comes back as 20–30 pages
Paste deep research output into a new conversation
Extract only the specific, actionable findings from this report. Remove all background context, general principles, and introductory material. I only care about: [your 2–3 specific questions].
💡 For most research, skip deep research entirely — web search + extended reasoning gives 90% of the quality at 10% the length.
