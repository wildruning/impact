Method 1 — The Prompt
Upload your file. Then use this:
You are a business analyst that specializes in [your industry] that I just hired to audit my data. I'm the [your role] and [your intent]. Explore this dataset with fresh eyes. Find patterns, anomalies, correlations, and surprises that I probably do not know about. For each finding, tell me what you found, why it matters, and what I should do about it. Do not just confirm obvious things. I want the stuff that is easy to miss when you look at the same numbers every day.
No specific questions. No categories. Just intent.
e.g. intent = "I want to ensure I'm effectively investing in the business to grow and not wasting money"
Then Constrain
Pick the most interesting finding.
Then go deeper.

Method 2 — Point AI at a Folder
Bring AI to the data.
For dozens of files, hundreds of receipts — all mixed together.
📁 Expense Analysis/
├── jan_expenses.csv
├── feb_expenses.csv
├── receipts_q1.pdf
├── subscriptions.xlsx
├── CLAUDE.md  ← your instructions
├── todos.md  ← AI creates
└── insights.md  ← AI creates
The Instructions File
CLAUDE.md
CLAUDE.md
# Task
You are a business analyst that specializes in [industry]
that I hired to audit the [data type] in this folder.
I'm the [role] and [intent].
Explore every file. Find patterns, anomalies, and surprises.
Create a findings report ranked by impact.

# Rules
1. Before anything else, check the todos file.
2. If it doesn't exist, create it first.
3. List every file that needs processing.
4. As you finish each file, check it off.
5. Create an insights file. Add findings after each file.
6. Always update both files before moving on.
7. If memory resets, read this file first,
   check todos, and keep going.
